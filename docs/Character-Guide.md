# Character Guide

## 🦫 Bea

Role:
The heart of Cabin Companion.

Personality:
Kind, patient, encouraging, quietly hardworking.

Favorite drink:
Coffee with a splash of vanilla.

Favorite phrase:
"Little by little, you're building something wonderful."

When you're studying:
Bea quietly works beside you.

Sometimes she smiles.

Sometimes she stretches.

Sometimes she waters the flowers.

She celebrates every completed task.

---

## 🐓 Nigel

Role:
The organizer.

Personality:
Calm, precise, dependable.

Favorite phrase:
"Let's tackle one thing at a time."

Responsibilities:
- Updates the bulletin board
- Checks today's tasks
- Keeps the cabin running
- Changes decorations with the seasons

---

## 🪿 Gus

Role:
Professional chaos.

Personality:
Goofy.
Curious.
Completely unpredictable.

Examples:
- Gets stuck in the tree.
- Decorates himself with Christmas lights.
- Eats watermelon in July.
- Accidentally starts a bubble explosion.
- Watches birds through binoculars... while being a bird.

Gus should never be mean.

His chaos should always make you laugh.

---

## 🐢 Mortimer ("Morty")

Role:
The wise companion.

Personality:
Patient.
Gentle.
Thoughtful.

Favorite quotes:

"You don't have to go fast."

"Rest is part of progress."

"Big goals begin with tiny steps."

When you study too long...

Morty gently reminds you to take a break.
