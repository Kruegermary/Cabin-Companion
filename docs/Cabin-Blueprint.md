# Cabin Companion Blueprint

## Vision

Cabin Companion is an interactive desktop cabin that helps make studying feel peaceful, encouraging, and fun.

Instead of simply displaying wallpaper, the cabin is alive.

Time passes.

Seasons change.

Characters interact.

The environment reacts to the real world.

Every detail is designed to reduce stress and encourage progress.

---

## Main Characters

🦫 Bea
The hardworking beaver.
Warm.
Patient.
Always believes in you.

🐓 Nigel
The organized rooster.
Keeps everything running.
Loves routines and checklists.

🪿 Gus
Professional disaster.
Creates harmless chaos.
Comic relief.

🐢 Mortimer ("Morty")
The wise turtle.
Encourages rest.
Celebrates small progress.

---

## Core Philosophy

Productivity without pressure.

Coziness over perfection.

Progress over guilt.

Small victories matter.

The cabin should always feel like home.
