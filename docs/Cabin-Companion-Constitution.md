# 🌲 Cabin Companion Constitution

*"My Cozy Cabin for Getting Things Done...
One Log at a Time."*

---

## Why Cabin Companion Exists

Cabin Companion was created to make work, study, and everyday life feel a little less lonely.

It is not meant to pressure people.

It is not meant to compete for their attention.

It is a quiet place that reminds people they are making progress—even on difficult days.

Cabin Companion should always feel like coming home.

---

# Our Core Values

## 🌲 1. Cozy Before Productive

If a feature makes the cabin feel less peaceful,
it doesn't belong.

---

## ❤️ 2. Encourage, Never Shame

No guilt.

No streak anxiety.

No punishment.

The cabin celebrates progress,
no matter how small.

---

## 🪵 3. One Log at a Time

Big dreams are built one small step at a time.

Every assignment.

Every glass of water.

Every chapter.

Every victory.

They all matter.

---

## 😂 4. Joy is Productive

Laughter belongs in learning.

Gus exists to remind us that life doesn't have to be perfect.

---

## 🐢 5. Rest is Progress

Breaks are not failure.

Water is productive.

Stretching is productive.

Sleeping is productive.

Morty reminds us that slowing down is sometimes the fastest way forward.

---

## 🐓 6. Organization Creates Freedom

Nigel keeps the cabin running so your mind doesn't have to.

The goal isn't perfection.

The goal is peace.

---

## 🦫 7. Kindness Wins

Bea represents the heart of Cabin Companion.

Every interaction should leave someone feeling encouraged.

---

## Our Promise

Cabin Companion will never try to become louder than life.

It will simply be a peaceful companion waiting to welcome you home.

---

Welcome home.

🪵 One Log at a Time.
